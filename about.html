I am a North Carolina native, and grew up in Apex, NC where I attended Apex High School and ran Cross-Country, graduating in 2010

I attended undergrad at UNC-Chapel Hill where I majored in Math and minored in Religious Studies. During my time as a student I was an instructor at the local Mathenasium center, and was able to snag a summer internship at NASA - Goddard Space Flight Center. I graduated in 2014

Immediately afterwards I spent three months riding a bicycle across the country with the organization Bike & Build. Our route started in Charleston, SC and ended in Santa Cruz, CA. Aside from walking, bicycling is perhaps one of the most intimate way to explore a large cross section of the United States and I highly recommend it.

After making it to the west coast I lived in Reno, NV serving as a member of the Nevada Conservation Corps, an Americorps position where you help build and maintain trails and other outdoor facilities.

Shortly after finishing up in Nevada I moved to Austin, TX where I worked for the Private Equity Firm, Vista Equity Partners, as a Database Analyst for the newly established Buisness Equity Team. During this time I also got involved in the local bicycle community and volunteered regularly as a shop-coordinator at the Austin Yellow Bike Project.

Currently I am a Graduate Student in the Mathematics Department at NC State University. When I am able to I also try to volunteer with the Raleigh Community Kickstand, Raleigh's community bicycle cooperative.

I also have a cat, her name is Marceline.
