Mushroom and Leek Risotto

Serves about 3

Takes about 45 mins

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups broth (or boulion)
  • Olive Oil
  • 8 oz Bella or Crimini Mushrooms
  • Leeks (sub green onions)
  • 1 cup arborio rice
  • 1/4 cup white wine (or more broth)
  • 1 Tbsp Butter (optional)
  • Parmesan Cheese

Directions:

  1. In small pot heat up Broth to simmer and reduce to low heat
  2. In large pot cook sliced mushrooms in olive oil for few mins, season with salt and pepper and move to separate bowl when finished
  3. In large pot cook leeks in olive oil for few mins
  4. Add rice, stir to coat, and toast for about a minute
  5. Add wine and cook till liquid mostly absorbed
  6. Add broth into rice about 1/2 cup at a time, stir almost the whole time. Keep it at a slight simmer, but do not let it boil. Add more broth as it heats
  7. When rice is 'al dente', remove from heat, add butter, mushrooms, and as much cheese as you like
