Falafel

Serves about 3

Takes about 30 mins

Ingredients:

  • 1 16oz can of chickpeas
  • 1/2 cup onion
  • a few cloves of garlic
  • 1 Egg
  • Bread Crumbs (or Flour)
  • Cumin
  • Red Pepper Flakes or other spicy substitute
  • Salt and pepper
  • Parsley (optional)
  • Tzaiki / greek yogurt or other sauce for serving

Directions:

  1. Heat a good layer of oil in a pan (1/2 - 3/4 of an inch deep).
  2. Mix together everything except the Tzaiki.
  3. Use whatever method to mash the chickpeas, should be kinda pastelike. You can ues a fork, potato masher, etc.
  4. Roll into small balls should be coheasive, smoosh into patties, fry in oil, flipping when one side is crispy. Be careful not to crowd the pan
  5. You may need to adjust heat as you cook / add more oil.

Source: My friend Suzette. She showed me how to make this in a generous stranger's kitchen during Bike & Build
