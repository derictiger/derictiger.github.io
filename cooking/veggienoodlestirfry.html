Vegetable Stir-Fry Noodles

Serves about 6

Takes about 30 mins

Ingredients:

  • vegetable oil
  • 1/2 head purple cabbage
  • 2 green bell peppers
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 2 3 oz bricks ramen noodles
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 3 Tbsp brown sugar
  • 2 Tbsp Sriracha
  • 1 Tbsp cornstarch
  • 3 green onions
  • cilantro if desired

Directions:

  1. Core and chop up cabbage into strips. Chop up peppers, carrots, and onion as well.
  2. Cook 2 bricks of ramen as per directions. Drain when done.
  3. Prepare sauce by mixing soy sauce, sugard, and Sriracha in small bowl.
  4. Heat up oil in large skillet/pot and saute veggies for a few minutes.
  5. Add vegetable sauce and saute until sauce is thickened and coated all veggies.
  6. Turn off heat, add noodles and toss to combine.
  7. Top with the green onions and cilantro.
