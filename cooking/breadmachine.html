Simple Bread Machine Loaf

Makes one loaf

Takes about 2.5 hours

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup hot water
  • 3 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups white flour
  • 2 teaspoons bread machine yeast

Directions:

  1. Pour water into bread machine pan.
  2. Add the sugar, oil, and salt to the water.
  3. Add the flour to the water, but do not mix.
  4. Make a crater in the flour and place the yeast in the crater.
  5. Follow instructions on your bread machine maker to knead the bread.
  6. Remove the dough onto a floured surface and punch down with your hands.
  7. Continue to knead the dough for a bit.
  8. When ready, roll the dough and place it in a greased bread pan seam side down.
  9. Make a lengthwise cut in the top of the bread about an inch away from the ends.
  10. Let rise for 30 mins and preheat the oven to 350 ° F.
  11. Place in oven and cook for 30 mins.
  12. Remove from oven and let stand till cool.

Source: Extremely Soft White Bread (Bread Machine)
