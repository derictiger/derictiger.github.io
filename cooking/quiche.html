Quiche

Serves about 4

Takes about 30 mins

Ingredients:

  • Pie Crust (store bought or make your own)
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (4oz)
  • Various veggies / meats (I like spinach, green onion, tomato, mushrooms, etc)

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 450 ° F
  2. In large bowel beat 3 eggs, add sour cream, cheese, and veggie / meat fillings (make sure to cook filling beforehand if needed)
  3. Pour mixture into pie crust
  4. Line edge of crust with aluminum foil and bake for 20 mins, then 5 mins without foil
  5. Remove from oven and let stand for 5 mins
