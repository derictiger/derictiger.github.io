No-Knead Bread

Makes 1 1/2 lb loaf

Takes about 1 hour 30 mins + 20 hours resting

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon instant (bread machine) yeast
  • 1 1/4 teaspoons salt
  • Some cornmeal for powdering

Directions:

  1. In large bowl combine flour, yeast, and salt. Add 1 5/8 cups of water, stir until shaggy and sticky dough, cover in plastic wrap. Let dough rest for 18 hours (at least 12) at room temp.
  2. When surface of dough has bubbles, lightly flour a work surface place dough, and fold over on itself once or twice. Cover with plastic wrape and let rest 15 mins.
  3. Shape dough into quick ball, coat cotton towel with cornmeal or flour and put dough on seam side down. Cover with other towel and let rise for 2 hours. When ready dough will have doubled and will not readily spring back when poked with finger.
  4. Half an hour before ready heat oven to 450 degrees, put oven pot in oven as it heats. When dough is ready turn dough into pot, seam side up. Shake pot so evenly distributed, cover pot and bake 30 mins, then remove lid and bake 15-30 more minutes, cool loaf on a rack.
