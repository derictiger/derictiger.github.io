Lentil Stew

Serves about 4

Takes about 1 hour

Ingredients:

  • Olive Oil
  • 2 Large Yellow Onions
  • 1 Carrot
  • 1/2 teaspoon thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon marjoram
  • 3 cups stock (or use boullion)
  • 1 cup lentils
  • salt
  • 1/4 cup parsley
  • 16 oz can tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup sherry
  • Swiss Cheese

Directions:

  1. Chop up Onions and Carrots
  2. Heat oil in large pot and saute onions and carrot until soft (~3 to 5 mins)
  3. Add thyme, and marjoram and saute 1 min
  4. Add stock, lentils, salt parsley, and tomatoes. Cook covered until lentils are tender (~45 mins)
  5. Add the sherry
  6. Add cheese while serving

Source: "Frances Moore Lappe's Lentils Monastary Style"
