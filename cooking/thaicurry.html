Thai Curry

Serves about 4

Takes about 30 mins

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of uncooked rice
  • Cooking oil
  • Whatever vegetables you'd like (Maybe onions, peppers, cauliflower, carrots, mushrooms, etc.)
  • 1 16 oz can of Coconut Milk (Full fat)
  • 2-4 oz Thai Red Curry Paste

Directions:

  1. Start to cook rice however you normally do it.
  2. Heat up oil in a pot and begin cooking vegetables, probably one at a time in order of what will take longest to cook.
  3. After veggies are sufficiently cooked add whatever spices you like along with the Thai curry paste.
  4. Cook a tad longer, then add in Coconut milk.
  5. After coconut milk has heated up to your desired level, serve over rice.
